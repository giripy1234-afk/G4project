Sensor Control on Tarred Road for Traffic Light Navigation

Welcome to our project on sensor control for traffic light navigation on tarred roads. This project aims to enhance traffic management and safety by utilizing advanced sensor technologies to optimize traffic light operations.

Our system uses a combination of sensors, including cameras, LIDAR, and radar, to monitor traffic flow and adjust traffic light timings accordingly. This helps reduce congestion, improve fuel efficiency, and enhance overall road safety.

How It Works

Our traffic management solution collects live road data, analyzes vehicle movement, and adjusts signal timing dynamically to keep traffic flowing safely and efficiently.

  • Data collection: Sensors detect vehicle presence, speed, and traffic density.
  • Intelligent control: Adaptive algorithms optimize signal timing in real time.
  • Safety focus: Priority is given to reducing congestion and lowering accident risk.

Why This Matters

By improving traffic signal responsiveness, this project makes road travel smoother, safer, and more efficient for drivers, cyclists, and pedestrians alike.

  • Reduced congestion: fewer delays and shorter commutes.
  • Lower emissions: less idling means cleaner air.
  • Improved safety: smarter signals reduce accident risk.
